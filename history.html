Our History & Culture

Learn about MIMAROPA's heritage

History

MIMAROPA has a rich history influenced by indigenous cultures, Spanish colonization, and maritime trade. Mindoro is home to the Mangyan people, known for their weaving and cultural traditions.

Palawan has been a trading hub for centuries, connecting the Philippines to Borneo and China, which shaped its diverse cultural landscape.

Marinduque and Romblon have preserved Spanish-era architecture and traditional crafts, showcasing centuries of history and artistry.

Festivals & Traditions

  • Moriones Festival (Marinduque)
  • Baragatan Festival (Romblon)
  • Palawan Festival of the Arts

Traditional Crafts & Foods

  • Romblon Marble carving
  • Mangyan weaving (Mindoro)
  • Seafood specialties (Palawan)
  • Kakanin rice cakes (Marinduque)
