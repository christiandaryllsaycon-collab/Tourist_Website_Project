Popular Destinations

Explore the must-see places in MIMAROPA

Top Tourist Spots

Puerto Princesa Underground River

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this underground river stretches for over 8 kilometers and offers a unique boat adventure through stunning limestone formations.

Location: Palawan

Coron Island

Famous for its crystal-clear lakes and WWII shipwrecks, Coron is a paradise for divers and nature lovers alike.

Location: Palawan

Marinduque’s Moriones Festival

This colorful festival celebrates the Holy Week with traditional costumes and street parades, attracting thousands of visitors annually.

Location: Marinduque

Romblon Marble Capital

Romblon is known for its world-class marble products and serene beaches, perfect for art enthusiasts and travelers alike.

Location: Romblon

Mindoro’s Apo Reef

The second largest contiguous coral reef in the world, Apo Reef is ideal for diving, snorkeling, and witnessing marine biodiversity.

Location: Mindoro

El Nido Beaches

Known for limestone cliffs and white sand beaches, El Nido is perfect for island hopping, kayaking, and unforgettable sunsets.

Location: Palawan
